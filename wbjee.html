WBJEE or West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination is a state-level entrance exam for colleges in West Bengal. WBJEE Exam Pattern

WBJEE 2022 Exam Pattern

WBJEEB has been conducting WBJEE in offline mode only. Candidates must keep in mind that WBJEE is held as an OMR-based examination, wherein, candidates are required to select the right answer by darkening the appropriate circle completely on the response sheet with a blue/black ball point pen. WBJEE 2022 consist of 2 papers, each allotted a different time slot on the same day. Each paper is held for 2 hours.

Exam Mode Offline (OMR based)
Type of Questions Multiple Choice Questions
Number of Papers 2 [ Paper-I (Mathematics) and Paper-II (Physics & Chemistry)
Exam Duration 2 hours for each paper (total 4 hours)
Exam Shift Paper-I (Morning Shift - 11:00 to 13:00) & Paper-II (Afternoon Shift - 14:00 to 16:00)
Number of Questions Physics - 40 questions, Chemistry - 40 questions, Mathematics - 75 questions
Mode of answering4 alternative answers for each question
Total marks200 marks

WBJEE 2022 Counselling :

WBJEE counselling will be conducted separately for every successfully qualified candidate. The online registration for the counselling procedure will begin after the declaration of result. Seats of various colleges will be allotted to the candidates in various rounds as per their ranks in the Merit List of WBJEE 2022.


WBJEEB usually conducts two rounds of WBJEE counselling followed by a mop-up round. WBJEE rank holders will be able to participate in all three rounds of WBJEE counselling 2022 while JEE Main rank holders will be able to participate in only one round (by paying a fee of Rs. 500).


For a candidate to gain admission in their preferred course and institute, WBJEE 2022 Counselling procedures can be divided into 4 major steps. After the final step, document verification of candidates will be conducted at the reporting centres.

The steps of the counselling process are briefed below.

  • Step 1 :

    Aspiring candidates who have either qualified WBJEE 2022 or JEE Main 2022 will need to register themselves for the counselling process. The registration can be done only on the official site i.e. wbjee.nic.in by filling in the details required. On successful registration, candidates will be provided with the ID & Password which has to be noted & kept safely for future use.

  • Step 2

    The next step to counselling after successful registration is payment of INR 500 as WBJEE Counselling Fees.

  • Step 3:

    After the payment is made, candidates will be provided with the opportunity to choose institutes for seat allotment. Candidates should be very cautious while Filling and Locking choices of institutes. It is advised to select the maximum possible number of institutes/branches (at least 20). This ensures seat allotment.

  • Step 4:

    Once the institutes are selected, the candidates will be alloted seats based on their ranks & preference. The candidates will be able to check their allocated seats on the official site by logging into their accounts. After the allocation of seats the candidates will be provided with the facility to - Accept the seat Accept with the possibility of upgrading to higher choices D0 nothing In case of acceptance, the candidate will need to pay the seat acceptance fee (â‚¹5000 for allotment in Govt.   • Step 5:

    Upon making the payment, the Virtual Reporting Centres (VRCs) will verify the documents uploaded by the candidates & will intimate the candidate about the status of approval/rejection SMS. The candidate will then have to log in & check the reason for rejection. The facility to re-upload the rejected document will be provided for a certain amount of time. Candidates can also contact the Central Control Room of WBJEE for any help required.


Date and time of the counselling cannot be changed under any circumstances. All the certificates and documents of the candidate will be verified at the counselling centre on the stipulated date. The candidate should appear for Counselling procedure himself/herself with all the necessary documents.
